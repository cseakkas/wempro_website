    FAQs

    How to apply?

    To apply, review our current openings to find a role that aligns with your skills and expertise. Start by providing your email address. After verification, you'll receive a link to proceed with your application.

    What are the steps involved?

    1. Initial assessment: After verifying your email, you'll participate in an initial assessment. This is a one-time, time-limited process. Successful completion allows you to upload your resume; failure to complete disqualifies your application. 2. Task or onsite visit: Depending on the job's nature, you may receive a task to complete or an invitation for an onsite visit. Your task performance and resume review determine if you move to the next stage. 3. Face-to-face interview: This step typically involves a personal interview. If you perform exceptionally well compared to other candidates, a job offer may follow within a few days, though exceptions can occur.

    What is the timeline for the application process to begin?

    The timeline to initiate the application process varies, typically ranging from 1 to 3 months, influenced by the number of applications. Roles that remain continuously open may experience extended processing times.
